Overview

    Package 'core' is the imp standard library. It is 
    structured as follows:

    /core/* ... a collection of generic functions 
    widely usable 


    /core/io.
            /File
                 /open
                 /close
                 /readCharacter
                 /readLine
                 /read
                 /write
            /stdin
            /stdout
    /core/control
                 /if.
                 /while.
    /core/crypto
    /core/func.
              /map
              /reduce
              /foldl
              /foldr
              /filter
    /core/container
                   /list.
                   /vector.
                   /set.
                   /stack.
                   /bst.
                   /bsa.
                   /hashmap.
    /core/net
    /core/sync
              /mutex.
    /core/os
