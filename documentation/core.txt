Overview

    Package 'core' is the imp standard library. It is 
    structured as follows:

    /core/* ... a collection of generic functions 
    widely usable 


    /core/io
            /print.
            /printf.
            /println.
    /core/control
    /core/crypto
    /core/func
              /map.
              /reduce.
              /foldl.
              /foldr.
              /filter.
    /core/container
                   /list.
                   /vector.
                   /set.
                   /stack.
                   /bst.
                   /bsa.
                   /hashmap.
    /core/net
    /core/sync
              /mutex.
    /core/os
